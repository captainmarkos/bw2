Blue Wild Scuba

Scuba Diving, Snorkeling & Freediving Charters

When we have a passion for something, as long as we never give up we can materialize anything. Our passion is the ocean and the marine life. We are always learning something new and greatly enjoy sharing experiences with others.

We're dedicated to excellence in service and safety. Our enjoyment comes from being able to share scuba diving, snorkeling and freediving experinces with all that are interested.

It's been a long and hard road to get here but with a lot of hard work and never giving up, we've done it! There are so many people that have helped along the way. To those that have helped (you know who you are), we are humbled by our lack of human resources in that the most we have to offer in return is our most heartfelt gratitude. Thank you and see y'all out there on the water!

Mark Brettin

USCG Captain and PADI Instructor

Born and raised in Michigan, around the Great Lakes, Mark has been around water all his life. He was Open Water certified in 1998 and became a PADI instructor thereafter. Now he enjoys exploring and diving around South Florida and the Florida Keys.

Austin Arrington

USCG Captain and Chief Engineer

From Woodstock, GA, Whitewater raft guide, ocean kayak guide, boatyard foreman, avid sailor and diver. Austin has a degree in Military History from the University of North Georgia but prefers to spend his time knocking around in small craft somewhere offshore.

Abby

Divemaster

Originally from Kansas City, Missouri, Abby moved to South Florida to pursue a Master's in Marine biology. She is a divemaster with too many dives to count. She was a mermaid in a past life and continues to spend as much time under the water as possible.

Sarma

Huntress and loving sweetheart

Rescued in New Mexico from the Los Alamos animal shelter at 5 months old, Sarma is quite the mixed breed. Sarma was found roaming in the Jemez mountains in the middle of winter. Now she's part of the family and loves the walks, chasing squirrels, iguanas and chewing on coconuts.
