North Miami Scuba Diving, Snorkeling & Freediving Charters

Just off shore of Miami are beautiful reefs and spectacular wrecks. In depths as shallow as 20ft and up to 140ft there are many magnificiant dives sites to explore. The water temperature ranges from ~70 degrees in the winter to ~85 degrees in the summer and makes it comfortable to dive all year round.

The majority of our dive trips consist of two sites where each site is unique in it's own way. A dive trip could be two reef dives, a wreck & a reef or two wrecks. Visit our charter schedule to see what we have planned. We run dive trips every day weather permitting. We are happy to accomodate special requests for charters and activities.

All experience levels are welcome here at Blue Wild Scuba and we will make accomodations based upon those experience levels. Please let us know if there are any special needs.
